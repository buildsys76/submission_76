When you run this code, it will generate a list of 336 lines in the format "YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM,VALUE". Each line represents an hour of electricity consumption for the specified building on January 1st to December 31st, 2016. The values are generated randomly based on the summer mean kWh, winter mean kWh, or yearly mean kWh, and never go below `yearly_min_kwh` or above `yearly_max_kwh`.
